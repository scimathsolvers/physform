Benign Beginnings

Physium Formulae began as a simple want for a faster way to do Physics homework, so @PhysForm decided to build some solvers. These solvers were originally written in CP-Basic and were extremely slow and heavy on weight. The original solvers may be requested personally. Since they were so heavy, @PhysForm decided to turn to Hollyhock-2, which had a miserable failure. Therefore, @PhysForm tried C#, fairing better and not returning to the project until the release of Hollyhock-3. The result is a now-in-progress project. Branching off into Mathnasium Calculae
